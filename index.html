CSIR-Imtech, Chandigarh
Industrial Visit
CSIR-Imtech, Chandigarh
Industrial Visit

Institution of Engineers, India (CSE Student Chapter) organised an industrial visit to CSIR-Imtech, Chandigarh on 6th Feb 2019. This visit is supervised by Faculty member Mr Pankaj Sharma. Students were introduced to the various biological processes carried on by microbes in day to day life, how microbial strains can be improved and their research department. Students were then taken to their MTCC building and IT room in Protein Building where all databases concerning microbes, proteins and genes are stored. The visit ended with a short address by the head. After this session students were provided with refreshments. At the end of the session there was a Q&A session regarding placements pre-requisites and after placement scenario in this field. Around 30 students visited CSIR-Imtech.

GitHub
Workshop
GitHub
Workshop

Institution of Engineers (India) Student Chapters' of Department of Computer Science and Engineering organized a 2 days’ workshop on GitHub. The workshop was attended by all the 3rd semester (Batch 2017) students of CSE. The workshop was presented by TopCoder Ambassador Mr Madhur Gupta and his team members. They explained to the students the need for GitHub in their curriculum, what exactly the GitHub is? GitHub is a web-based hosting service for version control using Git. It is mostly used for computer code. It offers all of the distributed version control and source code management functionality of Git as well as adding its features. The students were quite interested in learning this new technique of version control.
